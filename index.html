Trauma

Trauma results from experiences that overwhelm our ability to cope, affecting memory, emotions, bodily sensations, and relationships.

Depression

Depression can feel heavy or hopeless. Symptoms include persistent low mood, loss of interest, sleep or appetite changes, and difficulty concentrating.

Anxiety

Anxiety causes excessive worry, tension, or panic attacks. Breathing techniques and therapy can help.
